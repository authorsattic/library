The Biomechanical Analysis of Fast Bowling in Cricket



The aim of this study was to identify the key aspects of technique that characterise the fastest bowlers. Kinematic data were collected for 20 elite male fast bowlers with 11 kinematic parameters calculated, describing elements of fast bowling technique that have previously been linked to ball release speed. Four technique variables were identified as being the best predictors of ball release speed, explaining 74% of the observed variation in ball release speed.
