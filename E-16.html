Malory Towers

MALORY TOWERS

First Term at Mallory towers



Darrell and her friends grow together as they share their school days. There are new students to induct, sporting matches to be won, tempers to control and tricks to play on teachers. This work presents stories about life at boarding school as readers follow the girls' lives through 6 years at Malory Towers.



MALORY TOWERS

Second term at Mallory towers



Darrell Rivers is excited to be going into her second year at Malory Towers - but is she ready for the drama it will bring? Jealousy flares when a new head of the form is chosen, and the girls become suspicious when their belongings go missing. Who could the thief be? And why on earth has timid Mary-Lou ventured out during a terrible storm?



MALORY TOWERS

Third year at Mallory towers



The girls return for another term to find several new faces in their form. There is Zerelda, the American girl, Bill, short for Wilhelmina, who is mad about horses, and Mavis who has a remarkable voice



MALORY TOWERS

Upper fourth at Mallory towers



Darrell Rivers is delighted to be Head Girl of her form - and this year her sister Felicity joins Malory Towers too. Most of the time Darrell couldn't be prouder of Felicity, but she's much less keen on her sister's trouble-making new friend. With twins Connie and Ruth also causing problems, Darrell's first time in charge is a big challenge...

This work presents stories about life at boarding school as readers follow the girls' lives through 6 years at Malory Towers.



MALORY TOWERS

Last Term at Malory Towers



Last Term at Malory Towers is the sixth and the final book of the original Malory Towers series. In this final book about the girls at Malory Towers, Darrell becomes head girl and has to prepare to say goodbye to the school forever.



MALORY TOWERS

New Term at Malory Towers



Darrell has left, and Felicity is head of the third form! And what a lot she has to deal with - June and new girl Freddie playing tricks continually, the sickly Bonnie shadowing her, and the stuck-up Amy, who has a strange family secret...



MALORY TOWERS

Summer Term at Malory Towers



Summer Term at Malory Towers is the second book of the Malory Towers sequel, written by Pamela Cox. There's a mystery at the nearby stables - someone has stolen money and then poor Julie's horse. Can the third form help old girls Bill and Clarissa discover who is behind it?



MALORY TOWERS

Winter Term at Malory Towers



Susan is excited to be in charge of the winter concert, but Miss Tallant, a new teacher, has very strong ideas about it. And when she interferes in a midnight feast, they realise there's a spy in their midst. But who could it be?



MALORY TOWERS

Fun and Games at Mallory Towers



New girl Millicent clashes with the feisty June over the school's priorities: a music concert or tennis tournament? But both pale into insignificance when it's found there's a thief in the fifth form.



MALORY TOWERS

Secrets at Malory Towers



This book is filled with mischief, pranks , suspense , dangerous activities, excitement and the usuall excitement of malory towers books. If you come to the garden shed at 12p.m., everything will be revealed: Daffy's latest naughty prank, what Mam'zelle found in her handbag and Alice's amazing secret. The sixth form are in for a shock !



MALORY TOWERS

Goodbye Malory Towers



After taking their Higher Certificate, the sixth formers want to relax this term. But the Head has a surprise: a finishing school course, with old girl Gwendoline Lacey as teacher. Yet someone is determined to drive her out - and it takes the return of Darrell to solve the mystery.
